Artificial intelligence

Introduction

Artificial intelligence is an area of ​​computer science that aims to develop the practical and theoretical foundations for the development of computer systems that have intelligent characteristics and that generally correspond to intractable problems. Many problems that occur in industry, services and entertainment correspond to intelligent problems, and their solution is becoming increasingly indispensable in organizations due to the demands of competitiveness that increase every day. Among the various types of intelligent systems are: human-machine games, knowledge-based systems, optimization systems and machine learning.In this course, an introduction to artificial intelligence and its applications in industry, services and entertainment is made, and it is shown how the technologies based on artificial intelligence can create value and make organizations more competitive.

Course Teacher: Magister Hugo Vega Huerta

Course achievement

At the end of the course, the student will acquire general knowledge of the area of ​​artificial intelligence, design and implement human-machine competition games based on artificial intelligence and knowledge-based systems, making clear and precise use of search techniques in a space state and CommonKADS methodology.
